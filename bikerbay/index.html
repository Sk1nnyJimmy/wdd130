About Us

Our main purpose is to provide an "all-in-one" place for people to come in and get all the different types of information needed to ride around the Ogden trails. We were born and raised here in Ogden and spent much of our time riding along the trails on the mountains behind our house. We have come to love nature from biking. Rob (Owner) loves to spend his time with his lovely wife and their two dogs, Kakashi and Ahsoka, out in nature. He also likes to build model cars and play musical instruments in his free time.
